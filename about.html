Overview

I'm a Data Analyst based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a background in Mechanical Engineering and a strong passion for transforming raw data into meaningful insights. My journey began in the travel and administrative industries, where I developed analytical thinking, attention to detail, and a data-driven mindset. Today, I apply those skills to explore, clean, and visualize data for real-world solutions.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta, and certifications in Virtual Assistance (ALX Africa), Project Management (DEXA), and Independent Travel Management (DreamPort). I’m currently sharpening my technical edge through personal data analytics projects and continuous learning.

My mission is simple: to uncover insights that drive smarter decisions. Whether through Excel, Power BI, or Python, I enjoy turning messy datasets into clear, actionable stories that add value. Outside of work, I’m passionate about travel, innovation, and continuous self-improvement.
